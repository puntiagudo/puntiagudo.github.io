Tato
Walter Koch Rothach (1886-1962)
My grandfather from my mother’s side
By Jorge (George) Soini Koch
May, 2006
The information about Tato’s genealogy was given to me by my first cousin Pedro Koch who is currently living in Zurich, Switzerland.
The history of Puntiagudo farm was taken from the magazine Gaceta del Lago Todos los Santos, Dec. 2004.
Maps were obtained using the search engine Google.
Pictures were sent to me by my sister since she has the family photo albums.
As a result of the assignment given by our teacher (kumu) E. Kalani Flores for the Hawaii Culture 231 course in Fall 2005 I was able to get more informed about the significant life of my grandfather as well to know more about myself, my name and the incredibly beautiful mystery of Life. I hope to pass on this information to my children and eventually complement it with data from my father as well as from my wife’s side of the family.
It reinforces what was presented to us at the Kahilu Theater on May, 2005 by the students of Kanu o ka “Aina. Ho’i ka piko a malama i ke kahua…..Return to the source and maintain the foundation.
